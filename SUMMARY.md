# Mapping the Evidence Landscape

### Multi-Omics Integration and AI/ML in Lung Cancer: Strengths, Gaps, and Translational Opportunities

A systematic literature intelligence report covering **66,879 unique papers** across 12 themes, compiled from 213 queries across PubMed, Europe PMC, and Semantic Scholar.

**Vignesh Ravichandran** | Digital Health Institute, Rutgers University

Target Journal: *npj Precision Oncology* | April 2026

---

## How to Navigate This Report

This GitBook is organized into four parts:

1. **The Search** -- Scale, methodology, and what came back
2. **The Landscape** -- 12 thematic syntheses with cited evidence
3. **The Gaps** -- What no one has done yet (the review's strongest contribution)
4. **The Library** -- How to access and use the reference collection

Use the sidebar to jump to any theme or section.
